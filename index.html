Our Services

At Ultimate Dental, we offer a wide range of dental services including but not limited to the follwing.

Fillings

A treatment to restore the function, integrity, and morphology of missing tooth structure resulting from caries or external trauma.

Masking

Treatment done to block out the dark tooth so that the look can be restored to look natural and clean.

Cleaning

Part of oral hygiene and involves the removal of dental plaque with the intention of preventing cavities, gingivitis, and periodontal disease.

Bleaching

When used as directed, research studies have concluded that peroxide-containing whitening products are completely safe for tooth enamel.

Root Canal Treatment

A treatment sequence for the infected pulp of a tooth which results in the elimination of infection and the protection of the decontaminated tooth.

Teeth Extraction

Treatment that involves the removal of a tooth from its socket in the bone used for a tooth that has been broken or damaged by decay.

Bleeding Gums

Treatment for bleeding gums that can have causes that aren't due to underlying disease, Examples include flossing, teeth brushing etc.

Tooth Replacement

Crown, Bridge and Denture

Braces

Devices used in orthodontics that align and straighten teeth and help position them with regard to a person's bite, while also aiming to improve dental health.
